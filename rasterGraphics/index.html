Why I Created The Image?

I started this image with a picture of himself because I wanted that to be the base of the image. I tried to choose a good picture with a background so that the background could blend into a new one. The background I chose was somewhere in the Arizona Desert but it was perfect because the two hills on the side created kind of a arch for my picture. The background also blended into my orginal picture. After that I added stars because I really like the space and night sky. The last thing I did was I tried to add a picture of the city under me as if it was below me and I was standing on top which somewhat worked out. It was pretty hard trying to put myself into a new place but I think the picture came out pretty good.
