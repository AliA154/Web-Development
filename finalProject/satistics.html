The Statistics of Mental Health

Mental health is a significant issue for students and educators. It's important for schools to have a good understanding of the prevalence and impact of mental health problems among students. Here are some key statistics on mental health in schools:

Prevalence of Mental Health Problems

  • 1 in 5 children and adolescents have a mental health disorder.
  • 50% of all lifetime mental health disorders begin by age 14.
  • Suicide is the second leading cause of death among individuals aged 10-24.

Impact on Education

  • Students with mental health disorders are more likely to have lower grades and test scores.
  • They are also more likely to miss school and have a higher dropout rate.
  • Untreated mental health disorders can lead to increased risk of substance abuse, violence, and self-harm.
